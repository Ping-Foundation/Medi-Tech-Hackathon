About Hackathon

We aim to bring together hackers, dreamers and doers to solve problems related to medical field using existing technologies. The attendees work together to come up with innovative solutions that tackle multi-faceted issues in the medical field. Bringing together engineers and doctors to solve medical issues.

Who Can Attend

Do you have an idea to solve the problems in the medical sector with the help of advanced technology so that it will enhance people’s lives?

Anyone with an idea to solve a challenging issue faced in the health sector can attend.

Inviting medical and tech enthusiasts with an aim to facilitate technology in medical field to solve problems and foster a healthier society.

Mainly targeting university students and professionals to come up with prototypes for solving unmet medical needs.

Event Format

  • Participant Registration

    All the interested candidates should register. Registration Fee: 500
    A group of 3 persons can avail tickets at Rs 1300

  • Problem Statement

    Participants can either find a solution to any of the given problem statement or find solutions to unmet medical needs.

  • Idea Pitching

    Each team needs to present their ideas in front of judging panel.

  • Team Formation

    Participants can form a group with maximum 4 members, if needed.

  • Stimulate Novel Solution

    The teams will then get to work hacking through the issues associated with the problem they’re trying to solve — contributing factors, barriers, possible new solutions, or tweaks and refinements for existing practices. Create innovative prototypes and business models.

  • Showcase

    Present your work to an esteemed panel of judges for the opportunity to kickstart your idea and share your project on the big stage.
